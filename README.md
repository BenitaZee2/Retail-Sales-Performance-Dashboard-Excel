# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)

Analysis of retail sales performance across Nigeria — product category, regional, and sales team profitability — built using Excel PivotTables and PivotCharts.

## Overview
This project analyzes transaction-level retail sales data to uncover profitability trends across product lines, states, customer demographics, and individual sales rep performance.

## Dataset
~34,800 transaction records with the following fields:
`Date, Year, Month, Customer, Sales Person, Customer Age, Customer Age Group, Customer Gender, State, Product Category, Sub-Category, Payment Option, Quantity, Unit Cost, Unit Price, Total Cost, Total Revenue, Profit/Loss`

## Tools
Microsoft Excel — PivotTables, PivotCharts, calculated fields

## Key Insights
- Total profit across the dataset: **₦2,261,608.68** on **₦22,344,574.70** in revenue (~10% margin)
- Phones generated the highest revenue (₦11.49M), but Accessories delivered the highest profit (₦1.38M) — a margin story more than a volume one
- Adult customers drove the largest profit share (₦1.04M), followed by Youth (₦808K)
- Lagos and Abuja led all states by profit contribution
- Top-performing sales reps by total profit: Feyisola and Remota

## Repository Structure
- `Data Table/` — raw transaction-level dataset
- `Workings/` — PivotTable breakdowns by product, state, gender, age group, sales rep, and month
- `Dashboard/` — summary dashboard view
